Nearest MARTA Train Station Finder

This is meant to find the closest MARTA station to you. I decided to embark on this project because, in my experience, Google Maps doesn't allow one to directly search for the closest MARTA train station and get an accurate result.

Optional Settings

  • Free short-term parking
  • Walking directions (Note: Driving is the default setting)

Disclaimer: The program behind this page uses the spherical law of cosines to calculate the approximate straight-line distance between the coordinates reported by your browser/device and each MARTA station, then uses that information to find the closest station. In other words, this will get you to a station that is relatively close to you, but it may not be the absolute shortest driving/walking distance from you.
